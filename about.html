About us

What we do

We are a no-kill, volunteer-run dog shelter commited to proving loving, sincere care to dogs big and small, young and old. We care for, nurse, socialize, and love all our animals while they await adoption! If you are looking for a new furry friend, we warmly invite you to visit us!
